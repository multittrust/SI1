Call for contributions

We invite researchers and practitioners from psychology, cognitive science, computer science, AI, robotics, HCI, and HRI to contribute to our special issue on Trust in Human-AI Teams, aiming to explore the dynamics of trust within these teams across various contexts. Submissions will be peer-reviewed and selected based on relevance and quality of writing.

Submissions can include work in progress, extended abstracts, summaries of previous research, or new ideas.

Papers should be written in English and have 2-4 pages (excluding references). The review process is single-blind and the manuscript should not be anonymized. All submissions should use the ACM template available for LaTex and Microsoft Word. The LaTex template to follow is sample-sigconf, loaded with the nonacm class option.
